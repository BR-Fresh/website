Services & Pricing

BR Fresh offers on-demand grocery and local essentials delivery. Our platform is free to join for customers. We charge a nominal delivery fee based on distance, typically ranging from ₹10 to ₹40 per order.

Privacy Policy

Your security is our priority. We only collect essential data like your delivery address and name to ensure your orders reach you safely. We use industry-standard encryption for all data transmissions. No third-party sharing, ever.

Terms & Conditions

By using BR Fresh, you agree to our platform terms. We act as a bridge between local vendors and consumers. Users must provide accurate delivery information and adhere to fair usage policies.

Cancellation & Refund

Orders can be cancelled before store dispatch. Once dispatched, cancellations may not be eligible for a full refund. Refunds for damaged items are processed within 5-7 business days to the original payment method.

Shipping & Delivery

We offer hyperlocal delivery within a 5-10km radius. Standard delivery time is 30-60 minutes depending on store preparation. Delivery charges are calculated based on distance and shown at checkout.
